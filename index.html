We cater to all reading styles!

Kinaesthetic

A learner that needs to be actively engaged in their learning. They are tacticle, learners who use movement, testing, trial and error and a non-traditonal learning environment to retain and recall information.

Literary

A learner that takes in information primarily by reading and writing. They prefer to learn by reading notes and textbooks. These learners make use of dictionairies and other reference materials. They also benefit by rewriting notes and rereading notes silently again and again.

Auditory

A learner that assimilates by hearing and listening. They understand and remember things they have heard. They store information by the way it sounds and have an easier time understanding spoken instruction than written ones.

Visual

A learner that absorbs information through pictures or reading. They understand and remember things by sight. They can picture what they are learning in their head and learn best using methods that are primarily visual.
