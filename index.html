Love Reading

Dublin City Center Library

We cater to all reading styles!

Kinaesthetic

We read the book and bring the chapters to life using drama, song and performative poetry.

Literary

We summarise the chapters we have read into blogs. Each week a new member will contribute that week's summary on the groups' communal blogging website.

Auditory

We listen to the audiobook then come together to discuss the chapters. We share and welcome podcast and radio station recommendations that support our chosen novel.

Visual

We create mind maps and mood boards that reflect the tone and themes outlined in our stories.
